Pricing Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price you see in the calculator is exactly what you pay (plus tax). No travel fees, no "processing fees," no surprises.

What if weather delays my inspection?

We'll reschedule at no extra charge. You only pay when we successfully complete your inspection.

Do you offer discounts for multiple inspections?

Yes! Contact us for bulk pricing if you need regular inspections or multiple properties.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card, debit card, PayPal, and invoicing for commercial clients. Payment due after inspection is complete.
